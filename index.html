MY SCHOOL

Delhi Public School, Nalconagar, Angul, was founded in the year 1984 sponsored by National Aluminium Company and boasts of being only the third satellite school to be set up under the aegis of Delhi Public School Society. Situated 130 Kms away from Bhubaneswar in a serene and calm environment, the school lies sprawling in 30 acres of greenery with a multi purpose hall, auditorium, well stocked library, well equipped laboratories and play fields. A dedicated and efficient team of teachers work relentlessly to nurture and encourage the inherent talents of the students especially stressing on overall development of the child. Co curricular and extra curricular activities, sports and games, art, music and dance together with academics hone the skills of the students and prepare them to face the ever increasing challenges of a globalized world. In keeping with the motto of ‘Service before Self ’, students are encouraged to play active role in local orphanages and in assisting the less fortunate ones. The school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education with Science and Commerce stream and has just completed its 25th year and with student strength of more than 2000. It is no wonder that it attracts innumerable students from the vicinity and holds a place of an icon in the area.
